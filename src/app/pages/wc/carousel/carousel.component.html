First World Cup

The inaugural World Cup took place in 1930 in Uruguay, featuring 13 teams and marking the beginning of a global football tradition.

Most Recent World Cup

In 2022, Qatar hosted the World Cup, becoming the first Middle Eastern country to do so and delivering a tournament filled with memorable moments.

Upcoming World Cup

The 2026 World Cup will be co-hosted by the USA, Canada, and Mexico, uniting three nations to stage an expanded tournament with 48 teams.
